Error Log Submission Tracker Contact

Every correction request we receive is logged here and tracked through review. Submissions are anonymized — names, emails, and contact details are never published. Each entry shows the claimed issue, its category, and where it stands in the review process.

Confirmed corrections are published in full on the Error Log.
