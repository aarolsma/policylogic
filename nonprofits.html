A note on scope Nonprofit accountability raises distinct methodological questions. Unlike elected officials, nonprofits don't make promises to voters — they make commitments to donors, beneficiaries, and the public record. We're adapting our scoring rubric to reflect those differences. Our goal isn't to rank organizations politically, but to measure delivery against stated commitments.
